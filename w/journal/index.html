warp and weft

a misguided attempt to assemble knowledge about the unknowable

by reagan delafont, a fool

there exist beings fundamentally different to us.  greater than us.

they are not part of our universe the way we are.  they are integral to its existence.  if they were to be removed, the rest of the universe would unravel, individual threads falling into the void.

some call them gods.  they are not gods.


some call them demons.

i record them here.

Xeovo ash born

the ash born is one of the more bestial demons. its form is said to be lithe and quick, with a feline head and limbs that dissolve into smoke at the extremities. its size is inconsistent between reports, but i conjecture it to be around the size of a large autocarriage.

Xeovo is called ash-born, obscurer, lender. it has an affinity for lies, loopholes, and fluidity.

there are those who claim that Xeovo was born along with the first forms of life.

it was responsible for the fall of nevrä.

occultists attested to have dealt with Xeovo are listed below in reverse chronological order. if the outcome or an event related to the dealings is known or conjectured, i have noted it. dealings seem to happen periodically, once every 300-500 years.

mark odon circa 768 p.r.
li renvi c. 488 p.r.

the gray tarantula c. kreit 55-255
emperor hana c. maejn 320 the fall of nevrä
njatosec c. maejn 40 the resurrection (conj.)

Encolliate many surfaced

the many-surfaced cannot physically be described in any confidence. it is an ever shifting fractal, each supposed outer shell or skin shrinking into itself as a layer beneath springs into being. it floats above the ground, approximately the size of a one meter radius sphere.

Encolliate is called many surfaced, crusher, finder. it has an affinity for force, confusion, and the unexpected.

it is known to steal human children and turn them into monsters or heroes.

legends report that Encolliate was created by occultists in the late suhan dynasty

Vectra molten hearted

it of molten heart is the demon whose form we can be most confident in knowing. it has eight limbs, four of which are horse-like legs and four of which are human arms. its body is long, with little structure and great flexibility. its neck and face are human. instead of flesh, it is of superheated rock and earth, solid at the surface but not within.

Vectra is called molten hearted, mover, distrupter. it has an affinity for velocity, sinuousness, and seismic activity.

Vectra is the only demon which has been captured for any length of time.

occultists attested to have dealt with Vectra listed below. there is no clear pattern as to when dealings occur.

autopuppeteer Æorng

the autopuppeteer is roughly humanoid in shape, but is made of what seems to be polished wood. in size, it is reported to be larger than all but the tallest humans. Æorng has a vast number of arms, although the exact count has been reported as 7, 13, 14, and more than 100, variously. it has no face.

Æorng is called the autopuppeteer, ponderer, summoned. it has an affinity for manipulation and growth.

it repeatedly has set up convoluted series of events that lead to significant effects on the world.
